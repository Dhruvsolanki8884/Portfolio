### Design System

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #667eea (Blue)
- Secondary: #764ba2 (Purple)
- Accent: #10b981 (Emerald/Teal)
- Background: #0a0e27 (Deep Navy)
- Secondary BG: #1a2847 (Dark Blue)
- Text Primary: #ffffff (White)
- Text Secondary: #a1adc4 (Light Blue-Gray)

**Glassmorphism:**
- Background: Semi-transparent dark blue with 10-15% opacity
- Backdrop: Blur effect (10-15px)
- Border: 1px white with 8-15% opacity
- Creates stunning frosted glass appearance

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold, white, 3-6xl size
- Body: Light blue/gray, 1x line height
- Small text: Extra small, uppercase, tracking wider
